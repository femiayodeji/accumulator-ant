📊 Difficulty Progression Formulas

Target Sum

target = 10 + (level × 5) + (log(level + 1) × 10)

Starts at 10, grows logarithmically to keep difficulty curve smooth.

Number Range

maxValue = min(3 + floor(level / 5), 15)
range = { min: -maxValue, max: maxValue }

Expands every 5 levels, caps at ±15 for manageability.

Fall Speed

speed = 100 + min(level × 5, 300) px/s

Increases linearly, capped to remain playable.

Spawn Rate

rate = max(2000 - (level × 30), 800) ms

Numbers spawn faster as levels increase.

🐜 Ant State System

1. Seeking (C < T)

Condition: Current total is less than target
Visual: Small blue-tinted sack, wide eyes, forward lean
Speed: 100% (200 px/s)
Goal: Catch positive numbers to increase total

2. Balanced (C = T)

Condition: Current total equals target exactly
Visual: Green glowing sack, alert antennae, slight elevation
Speed: 90% (180 px/s)
Result: Level complete! Celebration animation

3. Burdened (C > T)

Condition: Current total exceeds target
Visual: Oversized red sack, drooping antennae, hunched posture
Speed: 50% (100 px/s)
Goal: Catch negative numbers to reduce total

🧘 Game Philosophy

"Life as a System of Choices"

The Ant: Represents the individual, the self navigating through life.

The Numbers: Positive numbers are opportunities and joys. Negative numbers are challenges and setbacks.

The Target: Your purpose, your ideal state, your goal.

The Lesson: True discipline is accepting only what aligns with your purpose—neither more nor less. Excess creates burden. Balance is mastery.
